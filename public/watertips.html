Water Tips

When using your dishwasher and washing machine, make sure to run full loads to maximize water efficiency. The water usage tracked will be the same regardless of how much you put in.
Collect and reuse water when possible. By reusing water, you won’t have to record another instance of water usage.
Limit the time you spend in the shower to conserve water. A shorter shower can save a substantial amount of water, especially if you use a low-flow showerhead. You can reduce the amount of time tracked on the shower form to reduce your overall water usage statistic.
Turn off the Tap: When washing your hands or brushing your teeth, turn off the tap while you're not actively using the water. This small change can save a substantial amount of water over time. You can record the reduced sink time when you record a sink usage.
Instead of using a fresh towel every day, consider reusing your bath towels a few times before washing them. This can significantly reduce laundry water usage and you won’t have to record an additional laundry use in the app.
Be mindful of water-intensive activities, such as running a hot bath or using the laundry machine too often. Use these sparingly so you won’t have to record uses anymore times than you need to.
